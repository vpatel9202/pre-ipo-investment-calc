Generic Pre-IPO Investment Return Model

A reusable calculator for private-company secondaries, SPVs, and feeder funds. It models entry valuation, exit valuation, dilution, fees, carry, annual drag, liquidation preference overhang, probability-weighted outcomes, and liquidity timing. Numbers are estimates, not tax or investment advice.

Interpretation guide

This tool cannot tell you whether the underlying company is good. It tells you how much company success you need to overcome valuation, dilution, fee drag, and capital-stack risk.
Model simplifications: annual management fee is approximated as NAV drag; carry is calculated on gains above returned capital plus optional hurdle; preference overhang is a crude haircut to exit value before your class participates; taxes are simplified. Real SPV/fund docs can differ materially.
